Frequently Asked Questions

The School Theme Competition is an event for the Sekolah Dian Harapan, Sekolah Lentera Harapan, Hope Intercultural School, and HORA Chinese School communities to express their understanding of the 2026/2027 school theme through the creation of a Theme Song and Theme Logo.

The School Theme for the 2026/2027 Academic Year is “Alive in Christ”, taken from Colossians 2:13–14.

This competition is open to the entire community of Sekolah Dian Harapan, Sekolah Lentera Harapan, Hope Intercultural School, and HORA Chinese School, including students in Grades 1–12, teachers, and staff.
